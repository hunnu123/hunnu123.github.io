About Chris

Early Life

Chris was born in Odessa, Texas the first of two boys born. Chris was born into a very reiligeous house hold due to the fact his father was sunday school teacher chris's father bought his son his first rifle, at 8 years old, a bolt-action.30-06 Springfield rifle, and later a shotgun, with which they hunted deer, pheasant, and quail. chris and his brother grew up raising up to 150 heard of cattle at a time. chris attended high school in Midlothian, Texas, and after graduating, became a professional bronco rodeo rider and ranch hand, but his professional rodeo career ended abruptly, when he severely injured his right arm.

Military life

After chris's arm got better, chris went to a military recruiting office, wanting in joining the U.S. Marine Corps special operations. A U.S. Navy recruiter convinced him to try, for the SEALs instead. at first, chris was rejected because of the pins in his arm, but he eventually received an invitation to the 24-week Basic Underwater Demolition SEAL school or for short BUDS, which he joined in 1999. chris was Assigned to SEAL Team 3, sniper element, platoon "Charlie" within the Naval Special Warfare Command, and with four tours of duty, chris served in many major battles of the Iraq War. His first long-range kill shot was taken during the initial invasion when he shot a woman, approaching a group of Marines while carrying a hand grenade. Because of his track record as a marksman during his deployment to Ramadi, the insurgents named Chris Shaitan Ar-Ramadi "The Devil of Ramadi", and put a $21,000 bounty on his head that was later increased to $80,000. They posted signs of the cross on his arm as a means of identifying him.
